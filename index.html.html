Every project here is built from real business questions — not textbook exercises. My background in trucking operations gives me both the data and the context to ask the right questions.

FLAGSHIP PROJECT — Built from my own real 2026 trip records

2026 Trip Operations Dashboard

In Progress

I am designing and building a complete SQL database from scratch using my actual 2026 trucking trip logs. This is not a practice dataset — it is real operational data that I structured, entered, and am now querying and visualizing to answer questions that matter to any carrier business owner.

The database tracks every trip: destination, origin, distance, date, day of week, load type, and revenue. I am building a full dashboard to answer these real business questions:

  • Which destinations are my most frequent routes — and are they also the most profitable?
  • What is my average trip distance, and how does distance affect revenue per mile?
  • Which day of the week is my busiest — and does busyness actually correlate with higher earnings?
  • What months and weeks had the highest operational volume in 2026?
  • Where am I losing money — long trips with low margins, or short trips with wasted deadhead miles?
  • What does my load mix look like — and which load types consistently perform best?
